Φatsalis

I am Alekos, also known as Φatsalis. My work explores the space where artificial intelligence, human emotion, mythology, and abstract imagination meet.

Working with data and algorithms shapes the way I understand complexity. Drawing allows me to translate that complexity into something raw, visual, and human. Each line becomes part of a system: part circuit, part memory, part dream. Through these works, neural structures, mythological figures, organic forms, and surreal machines merge into a language of controlled chaos.
